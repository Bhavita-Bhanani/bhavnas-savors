Our Story

One home kitchen.
One unwavering recipe book.

It started in 2003 in Bhavna Aunty's own kitchen on Tariq Road, frying her mother's samosa recipe for the neighbours every Sunday. Word travelled faster than the oil could heat — and a little tea-time hobby grew into Karachi's most trusted name for homemade savories.

Today, the same recipes are still hand-rolled, hand-mixed and fried in small batches every single morning — nothing is made ahead, frozen, or mass-produced. Every samosa is folded by hand. Every mixture is roasted, not just seasoned.

"We never set out to build a brand. We just refused to let the taste of home get lost."
